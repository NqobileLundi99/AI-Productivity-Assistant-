# README — Pilot AI Assistant for Small Businesses

## Overview

Pilot is an AI-powered workplace assistant designed to help small business owners automate everyday business tasks. The platform provides fast, professional, and user-friendly AI responses through a modern chat interface.

Pilot helps users:

* Generate professional emails
* Summarize meetings and notes
* Create task plans and schedules
* Answer basic business research questions

The goal of the project is to improve productivity, reduce repetitive administrative work, and provide affordable AI support for small businesses.

---

# Features

* AI-powered chat assistant

* Professional email generation
* Meeting summarization

* Business research assistance

# Problem Statement

Small business owners often struggle with:

* Time-consuming administrative tasks
* Writing professional emails
* Organizing meetings and plans
* Limited staff and resources

Pilot was developed to simplify these challenges using Artificial Intelligence.

Pilot combines multiple workplace productivity tools into one intelligent assistant. Users interact with the AI through a simple chat interface to complete daily business tasks quickly and efficiently.
